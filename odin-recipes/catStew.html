Savory Cat Stew

Delicous stew for those cold nights. All you need is one annoying house cat.

This stew is easy to make, and delicious. No one will ever know it uses an alternative source of protein!

Ingredeints

  • 1 annoying cat
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1 tablespoon calcium powder
  • 2 large eggs, lightly whisked
  • 1 fish, ground
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Situate the annoying cat so she can watch her subject cook her favorite meal
  2. Add all Ingredeints to the pot
  3. Simmer on medium heat until the fish is cooked through
  4. Let come to room temp before feeding to your annoying house cat
