Fish Tacos

Fish tacos are a delicious summer food. They can be made with wild caught, or store bought fish. A great way to mix up a fish fry!

Ingredeints

  • 4 filets of white fish of choice
  • 1 quart of oil for frying
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 large egg, lightly whisked
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Bread the fish
  2. Fry the fish
  3. Put in taco shells and hope you don't get food poisioning
